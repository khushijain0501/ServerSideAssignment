Mumbai, also known as Bombay, is the financial and commercial capital of India. It's a vibrant metropolis known for its iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India, historical museums, and Bollywood, the world's largest film industry. The city offers a unique blend of ancient traditions and modern life, with bustling bazaars, delicious street food, and stunning coastal views.
